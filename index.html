Key Research Publications

Featured Journal Articles

Weng, C., Kassaw, K., Tsai, P. S., & Lee, T. J. (2024). Scratch animation for SDGs with AI-comics: Impact on empathy, self-efficacy, and skills. Education and Information Technologies.

Abstract: This study explores the effectiveness of integrating Scratch animation with AI-generated comics to teach Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Findings show significant positive impacts on student empathy, self-efficacy, and digital creation skills, demonstrating a novel, technology-enhanced approach to global education.

Astatke, M., Weng, C., Yohannes, A., Isaac, M. M., & Liou, W. K. (2024). Immersive VR-based STREAM approach in education. Educational Technology Research and Development.

Abstract: This article presents a new framework for integrating Virtual Reality (VR) into the STREAM curriculum, showing how immersive environments can improve student engagement, spatial reasoning, and complex problem-solving abilities.

Puspitasari, D., Weng, C., Chu, R. J. C., & Chu, A. Z. C. (2024). Internet epistemic belief, digital literacy and social media engagement. Interactive Learning Environments.

Abstract: The research investigates the interplay between how students perceive knowledge on the internet (epistemic belief), their digital skills, and their active engagement with social media for learning purposes, identifying key factors for effective online interaction.
